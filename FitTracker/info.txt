Long description:

FitTracker is a clean, focused workout companion built for people who want to train consistently and see real progress.

Plan your workouts
Create custom workout plans with exercises, sets, reps, and rest timers. Schedule training days and start a live session with one tap.

Train with confidence
Follow guided workout sessions with rest countdowns, set logging, and haptic feedback so you stay in the zone from warm-up to finish.

Track what matters
Log body weight with a smooth drum-style picker, monitor daily calories and macros, and review your training history in a simple activity log.

See your progress
Watch streaks, charts, personal records, and analytics across week, month, and year views — all in a premium dark interface designed for clarity.

FitTracker keeps everything in one place: workouts, sessions, weight, calories, and progress — without clutter.
